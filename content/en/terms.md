These terms apply to venues participating in SentoVox and to respondents completing a SentoVox survey.

Participation is fully voluntary. Respondents can stop at any time. Venues can end participation in line with the terms of their participation agreement.

Respondents do not receive compensation for participating. Participating venues receive access to results and reports as set out in their agreement.
