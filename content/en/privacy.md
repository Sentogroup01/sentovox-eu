## Introduction

SentoVox respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data in the context of audience research for cultural venues.

## What data do we collect?

**Venue contacts:** first name, last name, email address and organisation name. Used solely to communicate about the study. Data can be changed or deleted at any time via [info@sentovox.eu](mailto:info@sentovox.eu).

**Survey respondents:** no personal data is collected. All surveys are fully anonymous.

## Voluntary participation

Taking part in a SentoVox survey is voluntary. Respondents can stop at any time without giving a reason. Because answers are anonymous, already-submitted responses cannot be retrieved.

## Security

All data is sent over a secure HTTPS/SSL connection and stored on servers within the European Economic Area. Access is limited to the academic team processing the data.

## Legal basis

Processing is based on consent and legitimate interest in audience research. The study complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## Your rights

You have the right to access, rectify, erase and object. Send your request to [info@sentovox.eu](mailto:info@sentovox.eu).

## World Land Trust

For every completed survey, 1 m² of rainforest is protected through World Land Trust. No personal data of respondents is shared in this process.

## Contact

Questions about this policy? Email [info@sentovox.eu](mailto:info@sentovox.eu).
