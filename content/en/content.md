nps_kicker: "Loyalty indicator"
nps_title: "Net Promoter Score (NPS)"
nps_body: |
  We ask cultural venue visitors whether they would recommend the cultural venue to family, friends, or colleagues. They give a score from **1** (would not recommend at all) to **10** (would highly recommend).

  Based on their responses, participants are categorised as **Promoters** (score 9-10, enthusiasts who encourage others to visit) or **Detractors** (score 1-6, those who would not recommend the cultural venue).
